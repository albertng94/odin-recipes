Paella Valenciana

The original Paella

Paella is probably Spain's most international dish, and there are lots of yummy versions of it. Below I present you the original version of spanish people favourite rice dish, coming straight out of Valencia.

Ingredients (for 2 people)

  • 250g of round grain rice
  • 250g of chicken
  • 250g of rabbit
  • 125g of green beans
  • 1 tomato
  • 0.75l of water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • A little branch of rosemary
  • saffron

Preparation

  1. Cut the rabbit and chicken into pieces, salt them and stir-fry them on the pan with olive oil. When it has some color, put all the meat on the pan's side, leaving its center empty-spaced.
  2. Grate the tomatoes and throw them into the pan's center. Cut the green beans and add them too. Add also the water and saffron, and cook everything with a medium heat for 18 to 20 minutes.
  3. Check the broth is tasty enough and correct the salt if needed. Add the rice and extend it along the pan. Cook with a hard heat for 6 to 8 minutes, and afterwards change to a soft-heat for 10 to 13 minutes. Before the last 5 minutes, add the rosemary branch into the pan.
  4. When the cooking time ends, take the pan out of the heat and leave it still for 5 to 8 minutes. After that, take the rosemary branch out.
  5. Serve and enjoy a delicious meal :P.
