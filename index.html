Spanish Cuisine

Spain is a country that counts with one of the richest cuisines in the world. Its different cultures and influences over the ages, have defined a variety of dishes that are well known within the whole Spanish territory, some of them even worldwide.

To celebrate Spanish gastronomy, I have selected some of the most well-known traditional dishes, that every Spanish food enthusiast should know how to cook. My hope is that you can do such delicious meals too!!
